About Me

I am currently an Assistant Researcher at Department of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University. I am also part of Group in Software Testing (GIST). My current research interest lies in the area of software engineering. Specifically, I am interested in solving software engineering problems by techniques that exploit artificial and human crowd intelligence.

I received my PhD degree from Nanjing University in June 2018, under the supervision of Professor Changhai Nie. I have been a visiting student at CREST of University College London (UCL), from September 2016 to August 2017. My supervisors in CREST are Dr. Justyna Petke and Professor Mark Harman.

Project

Combinatorial Testing Repository

Combinatorial testing is a potentially powerful testing technique to detect software faults. This repository aims to provide a full coverage of the publications in the literature of combinatorial testing.

CitHub

CitHub is an open marketplace for using and developing combinatorial testing tools. It aims to establish a software ecosystem of testing tools, underpinned by service-oriented and collective intelligence technologies.
